About Me

"My name is Sharina Stubbs and I’m a software developer. I'm also an experienced naturopathic doctor. I specialized in integrative primary care for six years prior to learning how to code. Every day, I felt like I was in battle with the system to not just achieve the best care for my patients, but to also survive in a broken medical system. I’m a software developer now so I can design and build solutions on a large, if not global scale, so I can tackle complex problems, and be on the cutting edge of new technologies. Solving problems creatively and proactively with a team, in a fast-moving environment brings out the best in me. I really enjoy writing clean and powerful code, and taking ownership for the project I’m part of. I’ve found that my background in identifying patterns within systems, my trained skills in communication, and my enjoyment of learning have all been a powerful asset to being a software developer and working with a team."
